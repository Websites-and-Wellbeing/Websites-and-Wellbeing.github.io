ABOUT

Websites & Wellbeing is a space where two worlds combine - creating and displaying bespoke websites, and taking care of wellbeing. These are strong interests and passions of mine; where creativity, innovation, growth, curiosity and problem-solving are at the forefront of my focus.

I promote the importance of looking after the mind, body and soul, and provide fun and engaging top-tips on ways to lead, and maintain, a healthy lifestyle.

Taking care of wellbeing also involves: engaging in activities and events that make you happy, having self-belief; and following through with plans, goals and projects that make you feel accomplished.

These are some of my accomplishments - delving into the digital world and encouraging good health. See my portfolio of website projects, and look out for useful wellbeing content and activities throughout this site; and on my social media channels!
